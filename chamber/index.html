🌿 Amazonía Empresarial 2025:

Jungle Business & Sustainability Expo Date: June 12–14, 2025 Location: Centro de Convenciones de Tarapoto, San Martín, Peru Hosted by: Cámara de Comercio de la Selva Peruana

🌱 Event Overview:

Join us in the heart of the Peruvian jungle for Amazonía Empresarial 2025, a three-day event that brings together entrepreneurs, community leaders, investors, and eco-innovators to celebrate and support sustainable business practices in the Amazon region.

🎯 Objectives:

Promote local businesses and indigenous entrepreneurs Highlight sustainable tourism, agriculture, and artisan industries Create networking opportunities between local and international stakeholders Discuss policies and strategies for responsible economic development in the Amazon.

🛍️ Event Features:

Green Market Expo: Over 80 local vendors showcasing Amazonian products (coffee, cacao, crafts, natural cosmetics, etc.) Business Workshops: Topics include exporting Amazonian products, digital marketing for rural areas, and sustainable business models Networking Roundtables: Connect with potential partners, NGOs, and investors Cultural Nights: Live music, traditional dances, and Amazonian cuisine tastings Ecotourism Fair: Promote jungle lodges, community tours, and conservation projects

🎤 Keynote Speakers:

Dr. Ana Ríos

– Economist & Sustainable Development

Advisor Julio Tapullima

– Indigenous Leader and Social Entrepreneur

Maria López

– CEO of Selva EcoPack

💼 Who Should Attend:

Local business owners and artisans Tourism and hospitality professionals Environmental NGOs and government reps Investors and international partners Students and researchers
