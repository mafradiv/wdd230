Chamber Site PLan

site name

Iquitos Chamber of Commerce

This name reflects the local community and its focus on supporting local businesses in Iquitos.

Site Purpose

  • To provide resources for local businesses, including networking opportunities and economic data.
  • To promote tourism and attract new businesses to the region.

Scenarios

  • What local events can businesses participate in to promote networking?
  • Where can I find resources for starting a business in Iquitos?
  • How can I join the Iquitos Chamber of Commerce?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Green) - Used for headers and navigation.

Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - Used for accents, buttons, and highlights.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto (for body text) - Readable and modern.

Secondary Font: Playfair Display (for headings) - Elegant and formal.
